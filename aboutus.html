About us

Welcome to SPADES, your ultimate tool in the fight against ocean pollution!
At SPADES, we believe in the power of technology to create positive change for our planet. With our innovative mobile application, we're tackling one of the biggest threats to our oceans: plastic pollution.
Our app utilizes cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to recognize and distinguish between good and bad packaging materials.
By simply scanning an item's packaging with your smartphone, SPADES instantly assesses its environmental impact, helping you make informed choices that contribute to a cleaner, healthier ocean.
With every scan, SPADES empowers individuals, businesses, and communities to take action against plastic pollution. Whether you're at the grocery store, a restaurant, or anywhere else, you can rely on SPADES to guide you towards sustainable choices that protect our marine ecosystems.
