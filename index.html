About SPADES

SPADES is dedicated to cleaning up the sea and protecting marine life. Through various apps and other technologies, we aim to make our oceans cleaner and safer for future generations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about marine pollution, with the simple app, and collaborate with governments and organizations to implement more sustainable solutions for ocean conservation.
